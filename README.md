# Hi, I'm Mariia 👋

I'm a builder and researcher working at the intersection of social-awareness and alignment of LLMs, and human-computer interaction. I recently finished my Master's in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, where I focused on making AI tutors more adaptive and personality-aware.

I'm fascinated by how models mirror human traits and interested in exploring exploring what that means what that means for reliability of real-world systems in high-stake contexts.

## What I'm up to

- Thinking about **personalized LLM behavior** at scale and how LLMs can become genuinely useful social agents rather than just chatbots
- Exploring **reliability and alignment** questions: when do models sandbag, sycophant, or deceive? And how do we even measure that?
- Building small tools that help me (and sometimes others) think better
- Occasionally getting pulled into product work, early-stage VC scouting, and hackathons

## Things I've made
**Technical:**
- [**rtai**](https://github.com/eremetech/rtai) — DeepPoly neural network verifier in PyTorch for certifying adversarial robustness (MNIST, CIFAR-10). Built for Reliable & Trustworthy AI at ETH; scored 112.5/115 on the test suite.
- [**yakut_tok**](https://github.com/eremetech/yakut_tok) — Custom Byte Pair Encoding tokenizer and fine-tuning pipeline for Yakut, a low-resource Turkic language.
- [**sentiment_challenge**](https://github.com/eremetech/sentiment_challenge) — BERTweet + CNN-RNN-Attention fusion network for noisy-label Twitter sentiment classification (92.12% on the public leaderboard).
- [**Probabilistic_AI**](https://github.com/eremetech/Probabilistic_AI) — Course projects: Bayesian inference, Gaussian Process regression, Bayesian neural networks, hyperparameter tuning, off-policy RL for pendulum swing-up.
- [**AI_in_Education_course**](https://github.com/eremetech/AI_in_Education_course) — Experiments with LLMs for education: pedagogical strategy selection, language complexity adaptation, tutoring pipelines.
- [**InteractiveML**](https://github.com/eremetech/InteractiveML) — Interactive tool that helps policymakers choose budget-constrained policies to maximize national happiness, using Gapminder data.

  
**For fun:**
